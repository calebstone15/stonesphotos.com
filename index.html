How the model works...

At Stone's Photos, I believe in providing high-quality photography services at an affordable price. Whether you're looking for engagement photos, senior portraits, or anything in between, I am here to capture your moments with the client at the forefront of my process.

  1. Photo Ownership - The photos I take belong to you, the client. You can use them for any purpose, and I will send you 100% of the photos I take including the raw files so you can edit them to your liking. This gives you thousands of high quality photos to do what you want while never adding a watermark.
  2. Editing Choice - Every shoot comes with 30 free edited photos of your choosing not mine. If there is one thing I have learned is that no two people like the exact same photos so I let you choose what gets edited not me.
  3. Pricing Simplified - The price is travel + $200/day and thats it. No charges for time, outfit changes, or any of the unnecessary things other photographers add on. The only thing that could make your shoot cost more is if you want me to edit more photos and that is just $2 per photo.
